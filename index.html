Resumen del perfil

We are seeking a highly skilled Maintenance Technician with a background in mechanical and mechatronics engineering and a minimum of two years’ hands-on experience in maintaining specialized machinery. This person will be responsible for ensuring optimal performance, diagnosing technical issues, suggesting improvements, and ensuring the continuous operation of critical equipment.

Requisitos clave

  • Technical or technological degree in Mechanical Engineering, Mechatronics, Industrial Maintenance, or a related field.
  • Minimum of 2 years of experience in industrial or specialized equipment maintenance.
  • Strong knowledge of electromechanical, pneumatic, and hydraulic systems.
  • Proficient in diagnosing, repairing, and optimizing complex, high-precision machines.
  • Skilled in reading and interpreting technical manuals, schematics, and electrical diagrams.
  • Experience using diagnostic tools, maintenance software (preferred), and generating technical reports.
  • Proactive, solution-oriented mindset with a strong commitment to continuous improvement.
  • Availability for both scheduled maintenance and emergency repairs as needed.
  • Bilingual in English and Spanish is a plus.

Responsabilidades clave

  • Perform preventive and corrective maintenance as per established schedules.
  • Diagnose and repair technical issues efficiently and safely.
  • Maintain detailed records of all maintenance activities for each piece of equipment.
  • Propose and implement technical improvements to optimize machinery performance.
  • Collaborate with the production team to minimize equipment downtime.
  • Ensure compliance with safety and industrial standards during all maintenance tasks.

Competencias principales

  • Technical and analytical thinking
  • Attention to detail
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Effective communication
  • Adaptability to new technologies and methodologies
